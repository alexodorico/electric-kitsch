• About

    Electric Kitsch is a vintage boutique that deals with items that are generally 25+ years old. This pertains to records, clothes, electronics, instruments, and household items. We also offer guitar and amplifier/electronics repair from old to new.

  • Records

    Here at Electric Kitsch we carry both new and used records! We stock samba, death metal, and everything in between. For new records, we go through a wide variety of distributors and labels to get you the best prices and best selections possible. If it's in print, we can get it! If you're looking to sell or trade records, we do that too!

  • Kitsch

    Besides our killer record collection, we also carry vintage clothes, electronics, guitar accessories, knick-knacks, and more! You never know what you might find at The Kitsch because we put something new out every single day! Come see what the fuss is all about!

  • Services

    We specialize in stringed instrument and electronics repair. We offer instrument setups, nut/bridge/fret work, guitar electronics work, and amplifier/electronics repair.
